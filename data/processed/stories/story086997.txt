(EW.com) -- No surprise, but now it's official: "The Hunger Games: Catching Fire" will have a panel at Comic-Con next month, Lionsgate announced today.
It's not clear who exactly from the movie will be in attendance (Paging Jennifer Lawrence!), but Lionsgate promises "talent" from the film will be there. The Lionsgate panel will also featuring actors from "I, Frankenstein, "which will star Aaron Eckhart, Yvonne Strahovski and Bill Nighy. The Saturday, July 20 panel will debut an exclusive new trailer for "Catching Fire" and new footage from "I, Frankenstein."
Not heading to San Diego? You can tweet questions for the panel using the hashtag #CatchingFireComicCon. EW will also be bringing you reports from of all the happenings at Comic-Con, which will take place July 18-21.
"The Hunger Games: Catching Fire" hits theaters Nov. 22.
