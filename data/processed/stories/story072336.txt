(EW.com) -- If there were one holiday that was tailor-made for sitting on the couch and indulging in a marathon of your favorite show, it would be Thanksgiving.
So if you find yourself unable to help in the kitchen or simply not interested in the parade — which starts at 9 a.m. on NBC and CBS — and/or football viewing parties around you, we've rounded up some of your other viewing options for the day.
And we're talking the entire day. It's TV marathon time!
