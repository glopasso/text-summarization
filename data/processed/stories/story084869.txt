DENVER, Colorado -- Massachusetts Democrat Edward Kennedy, recuperating from recent brain surgery, made a dramatic appearance at his party's national convention in Denver, Colorado on Monday.
Edward Kennedy's speech in Denver, Colorado, marked his second public appearance since brain surgery.
The senator pledged to see Barack Obama to the White House and his own return to the Senate floor.
Caroline Kennedy, daughter of slain President John F. Kennedy, introduced her ailing uncle.
