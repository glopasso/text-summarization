(EW.com) -- Rita Ora, best known as a singer signed to Jay Z's Roc Nation label, has been cast as Mia, Christian Grey's adopted sister, in "Fifty Shades of Grey."
The British pop star has only four IMDb credits to her name, so this will be a pretty high-profile introduction to her acting.
