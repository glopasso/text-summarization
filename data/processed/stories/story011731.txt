Face Time -- Mehdi Houas, Tunisian Minister of Tourism
The country that sparked the Arab Spring is on the road to recovery. Unlike its neighbors to the East and West however, Tunisia does not sit upon vast oil and gas reserves to aid in the post-revolutionary process.
MME sits down with Mehdi Houas, Tunisia's Minister of Culture and Tourism, to discuss his country's plans to reposition itself as a regional hub for business.
In Focus -- Tunisia tourism
Tunisia in the process of defining its post-revolution road map. Part of the plan aims to instigate domestic growth by repositioning the North African country in the global market.
MME looks at Tunisia's attempts to reinvigorate its international business ties.
