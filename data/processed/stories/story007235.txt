(EW.com) -- TNT's "Dallas" has snagged itself a Six Million Dollar Man.
EW has learned exclusively that Lee Majors has booked a two-episode guest spot on the second season of TNT's hit reboot drama.
He will play Ken Richards, an old flame of Sue Ellen's who finds himself crossing paths with her once again for business matters.

Majors, who is best known for playing Col. Steve Austin, recently had guest spots on "Raising Hope," "Grey's Anatomy" and "CSI: NY."
"Dallas" kicked off its second season on Tuesday, with an episode that notched almost 3 million viewers.
