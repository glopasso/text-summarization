(EW.com) -- There aren't really any rules when it comes to celebrating Labor Day. Some people like to enjoy their last days of summer with a beach trip, and others like to use their Monday off to never change out of their pajamas. We're not here to judge. But what we are here to do is give you your all-day TV marathon options, just in case you like to celebrate in front of the TV.
We've rounded up the marathons that will keep you busy from morning till night (none of this five-episodes-and-done stuff):
*Note: Final time is when final episode begins; all in ET
US Open Tennis (CBS, 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.)
Criminal Minds (ION, 11 a.m. to 2 a.m.)
