(EW.com) -- Nate Berkus, the interior designer discovered by Oprah in 2002, is engaged to Jeremiah Brent, his boyfriend of nearly a year.
People reports that the star of "The Nate Berkus Show," which aired from 2010 to 2012, proposed to Brent on Monday.
The couple then celebrated with friends in Peru.
