Actor Anthony Mackie was arrested early Saturday for drunken driving in New York City, police said.
The arrest happened at 1:22 a.m. in Harlem, Detective Brian Sessa told CNN.
Mackie's camp did not immediately return a call later that day seeking comment.
The celebrated actor in theater and television has starred in numerous films such as "The Hurt Locker," which won the best picture Academy Award in 2010.
