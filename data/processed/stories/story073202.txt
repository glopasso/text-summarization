(EW.com ) -- Kelly Ripa and Michael Strahan are headed to Washington.
On Tuesday's "Live! with Kelly and Michael," the co-hosts announced their first-ever interview with President Barack Obama. The duo is headed to D.C. to talk to Obama in the Blue Room of the White House, where they'll discuss the recent "White House Healthy Kids and Safe Sports Concussion Summit," among other topics.
The interview will air Friday, May 30, 2014.
