Argentine soccer legend Diego Maradona was taken to an area hospital after his vehicle and a bus collided, but he is fine, the state-run Telam news agency reported Monday.
Maradona was traveling with his girlfriend in Ezeiza, on the outskirts of Buenos Aires, when the accident happened, Telam said. Both were transported to a local hospital for observation, the news agency reported.
Sources close to Maradona told Telam neither Maradona nor his girlfriend suffered serious injuries.
Maradona is a former Argentine player and coach, revered by many as one of game's greatest players.
