Who -- or what -- will be the gang's next big foe? What, exactly, is "all that matters"? Where's Carl?

Otherwise, Rick and the surviving members of his crew are back together, apparently, roaming foggy woods, in pursuit of an unidentified enemy, if the trailer is any indication.
